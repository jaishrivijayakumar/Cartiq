# CartIQ — Smart Supermarket Shopping

CartIQ is an in-cart smart shopping web app that helps supermarket customers find products instantly, scan items as they shop, navigate aisles efficiently, and skip checkout queues — all from their phone mounted on the cart.

## Project Overview

CartIQ was built as part of the **Wadhwani Foundation Ignite Entrepreneurship Program** — a national-level startup incubation initiative. The product addresses real, everyday friction points that supermarket shoppers face: wasted time finding products, long checkout queues, forgotten items, and missed discount opportunities.

CartIQ turns the shopping cart itself into an intelligent assistant. The shopper mounts their phone on the cart, opens the app, and CartIQ handles everything from in-store navigation to express checkout — no tech expertise required.

---

## Problem Statement

Every week, millions of supermarket shoppers lose significant time and money to the same four recurring problems:

| Problem | Impact |
|---|---|
| Cannot find products | Average 23 minutes lost per trip wandering aisles |
| Checkout queue wait times | Up to 17 minutes lost per visit standing in line |
| Forgotten items | 1 in 3 shoppers forgets at least one key item per visit |
| Missed discounts and deals | Shoppers miss over ₹800 worth of available deals every month |

---

## Solution

CartIQ solves all four problems in a single, seamless app experience:

- **Smart Product Locator** eliminates wandering by pointing shoppers to the exact aisle, shelf, and position of any product
- **Barcode Scanning** lets shoppers add items to their digital cart as they go, with price and location info auto-filled
- **Store Navigation** shows the fastest route through the supermarket based on the shopping list
- **Express Checkout** generates a QR code at the end of the trip — shoppers scan at the exit lane and walk straight out, no queue required
- **Discount Alerts** surface real-time deals on products already in the cart, ensuring no savings are missed

## How It Works

1. **Mount and Connect** — Clip your phone to the cart holder at the store entrance. CartIQ auto-detects the store and loads the live product map.
2. **Scan Items As You Shop** — Point the camera at any barcode. Items are added to your cart with price and aisle info automatically.
3. **Navigate to Any Product** — Search any product name. The app highlights the exact aisle and shows the fastest route.
4. **Skip the Queue and Pay** — Generate a QR code, walk to the express exit, scan once, and done. Zero waiting.
